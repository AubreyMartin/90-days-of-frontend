The All New Volkswagen Taigun

Volkswagen has commenced production of the updated Taigun at its Pune facility, reinforcing its ‘Make in India, for India and the world’ strategy.
Built with a strong focus on localisation and precision manufacturing, the new Taigun continues to cater to Indian driving conditions while retaining Volkswagen’s signature European driving dynamics.
The latest iteration brings a refreshed design with sharper styling elements and an added dose of premium appeal, making the SUV look more contemporary and edgy.
Underneath, the Taigun stays true to its core strengths—balanced performance, ride comfort, and a solid safety package.
It continues to uphold its 5-star safety credentials, a key factor behind its popularity among Indian buyers.

Detailed Review

The new Volkswagen Taigun offers a balanced driving experience with strong performance and smooth handling. The engine feels responsive, especially in city traffic, while also delivering a stable ride on highways. Comfort is a strong point, with supportive seats and a well-built interior. The suspension handles rough roads well, making it suitable for Indian driving conditions.

Pros:

  • Solid build quality and safety
  • Smooth and stable driving experience
  • Premium interior feel

Cons:

  • Slightly expensive compared to rivals
  • Rear seat space could be better
  • Mileage is average
